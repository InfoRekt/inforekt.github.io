So, you want to contribute? Awesome! Your article can be in any style you prefer as long as you keep in mind that:

  • It has to be somehow about information security;
  • It needs to have advice on where they went wrong and what they should have done;
  • Sarcasm, condesending tone or funny writing style are optional but appreciated, we are not a very serious site.

    There are two obvious ways to do that:

    1. Submit a pull request

    The best way to submit an article is to create a pull request on our github repo. You do this by forking the repository and create a new article in the _posts directory. Keep in mind that the headers in the posts that are already there also need to be included in your article.

    If this is your first submission you should also create an author page. It's strongly suggested to not use any personal information in the author page, unless you prefer this.

    Once you have done this create a pull request and wait for feedback or merge.

    2. Email

    The other method is by email. You can email cpthindsight@inforekt.fail with your story. You will need to send us your story in txt or html format. This can be both inside the email or as uncompressed attachment.
    Zip, Rar, Word, LaTeX, PDF or any other format is not acceptable and will be ignored.


    Note: if you only want to submit a link to an article you want something written about you should hop over to our freenode #inforekt channel. You don't need an IRC client for this, you can just use the Freenode Webchat and fill in the channel edit box with #inforekt. Another option is to just send us a DM or tweet at our twitter 0xInfoRekt.
